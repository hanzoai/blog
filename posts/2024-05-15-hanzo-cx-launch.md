# Hanzo CX: Complete Customer Experience Platform

Customer support tools are fragmented. Email in one tool, chat in another, social media in a third. Your support team switches between five tabs to handle one customer who messaged on three channels about the same issue.

Today we are launching Hanzo CX: a unified customer experience platform.

## Unified Inbox

Every customer conversation in one place:

- **Email**: Full email support with threading
- **Live chat**: Website widget with real-time messaging
- **Social**: Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram DMs
- **Messaging**: WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS
- **Phone**: VoIP integration with call recording and transcription

One conversation thread per customer, regardless of channel. Switch channels mid-conversation without losing context.

## AI-Assisted Responses

AI that helps your team respond faster and more consistently:

- **Suggested replies**: AI drafts responses based on conversation context and knowledge base
- **Sentiment analysis**: Flag frustrated customers for priority handling
- **Auto-categorization**: Tickets tagged and routed automatically
- **Knowledge surfacing**: Relevant help articles shown to agents in real time

The AI assists. Your team decides. No automated responses without human approval (unless you configure it).

## Knowledge Base

Self-service support that actually works:

- Rich text articles with search
- Version-controlled content
- Analytics on what customers search for (and do not find)
- AI-powered article suggestions in chat widget

## Automation

Workflow automation for common support patterns:

- Auto-assignment based on topic, language, or customer tier
- SLA tracking with escalation rules
- CSAT surveys triggered after resolution
- Follow-up reminders

- **Free**: 2 agents, email and chat only
- **Team**: $19/agent/month — all channels, AI features
- **Business**: $39/agent/month — automation, analytics, SLA tracking
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing
