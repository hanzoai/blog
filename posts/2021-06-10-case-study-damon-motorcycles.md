# Case Study: Damon Motorcycles — 500x ROI Campaign

Damon Motorcycles builds electric motorcycles with AI-powered safety technology. They came to Hanzo with a specific challenge: create a marketing campaign that would generate measurable ROI for a product category — electric motorcycles — that most consumers did not yet understand.

## The Challenge

Electric motorcycles faced a double awareness problem: most consumers did not know electric motorcycles existed, and those who did assumed they were inferior to combustion bikes in performance and range. Damon needed to educate the market while generating leads that would convert to deposits.

## What We Did

**Audience segmentation**: We identified three distinct buyer personas — technology early adopters, environmentally conscious riders, and performance-focused motorcyclists — each requiring different messaging and channels.

**Content production**: Video content showcasing Damon's CoPilot safety system (the first advanced warning system for motorcycles) and HyperDrive electric powertrain. Technical content for enthusiasts, lifestyle content for broader audiences.

**Multi-channel execution**: Targeted advertising across YouTube, Instagram, and motorcycle-specific media. Influencer partnerships with respected riders and tech reviewers. SEO-optimized content targeting comparison searches.

**Conversion optimization**: Landing page A/B testing, deposit flow optimization, and retargeting sequences that addressed common objections (range anxiety, charging infrastructure, resale value).

## Results

- **500x return** on marketing investment
- **230% increase** in qualified leads
- **65% conversion rate** from lead to deposit
- **45% reduction** in customer acquisition cost
- Featured in top motorcycle and EV publications
