# Case Study: Bellabeat — Scaling a Women's Health Platform

Bellabeat makes health wearables designed specifically for women — tracking stress, menstrual cycles, sleep, and activity in a holistic wellness framework. Hanzo provided fractional CMO and CTO services, marketing automation, and data analytics to scale the platform from early traction to projected $35M revenue.

## The Challenge

The wearable market was dominated by general-purpose devices (Apple Watch, Fitbit) that treated women's health as an afterthought. Bellabeat had product-market fit with their core audience but needed the marketing infrastructure and data capabilities to scale without losing their brand identity.

## What We Did

**Fractional CMO/CTO services**: Strategic leadership for marketing and technology without the cost of full-time C-suite hires. We set the marketing strategy, managed the tech roadmap, and built the teams to execute both.

**Marketing automation**: Built automated lifecycle campaigns — onboarding sequences, engagement re-activation, subscription renewal — that maintained personal touch at scale. Email, push notification, and in-app messaging coordinated through a unified automation platform.

**Data analytics**: Customer behavior analysis, cohort retention tracking, and predictive churn modeling. We identified the key activation metrics that predicted long-term retention and optimized the user journey around them.

**Web design**: Rebuilt the e-commerce experience with conversion-optimized product pages, educational content hubs, and a community platform for Bellabeat users to share health journeys.

## Results

- **67% increase** in user engagement
- **$35M revenue projection** by 2024 (achieved)
- **3x improvement** in email campaign conversion rates
- **42% reduction** in subscriber churn
- **2x increase** in average order value through cross-sell optimization
