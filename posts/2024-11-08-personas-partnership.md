# Hanzo Partners with Personas Social to Power Keek's AI

Today we are announcing a strategic partnership with Personas Social Inc. to integrate Hanzo AI infrastructure into the Keek social media platform.

## The Partnership

Keek is expanding its social platform with AI-powered features including content recommendations, safety moderation, and personalized experiences. Hanzo will provide the AI infrastructure layer:

- **Content understanding**: Multimodal AI for analyzing video, image, and text content
- **Recommendation engine**: Personalized content feeds powered by our recommendation infrastructure (the descendant of EARLE, our original commerce recommendation engine)
- **Safety and moderation**: AI-powered content moderation with human-in-the-loop review
- **Creator tools**: AI-assisted content creation and editing capabilities

## Why Hanzo

Personas Social selected Hanzo for three reasons:

1. **Production experience**: We have run recommendation systems at scale since 2014. This is not a research project.
2. **Full stack**: LLM inference, compute infrastructure, and application tooling from a single provider. No integration tax.
3. **Cost efficiency**: Our inference infrastructure, built on Zen models and optimized serving, delivers AI capabilities at a fraction of hyperscaler pricing.

## What This Means

For Keek users: smarter feeds, better content discovery, and AI-powered creative tools — rolling out over the coming months.

For Hanzo: validation that our AI infrastructure serves social-scale workloads. The Keek integration will process millions of content items daily through our inference pipeline.

## Looking Forward

This partnership extends Hanzo's reach beyond commerce and developer tools into social media infrastructure. The AI capabilities are the same — content understanding, recommendation, generation — but the scale and latency requirements push our systems in valuable directions.
