# Case Study: Keek — AI-Powered Social Media Transformation

Personas Social partnered with Hanzo to transform Keek from a traditional social media platform into an AI-powered content discovery engine. The integration spans recommendation, moderation, and creator tools — all built on Hanzo AI infrastructure.

## The Challenge

Keek's chronological feed was losing engagement to algorithmically-driven competitors. Users spent less time discovering new content and creators struggled to reach new audiences. Meanwhile, content moderation at scale — millions of daily video, image, and text uploads — required an automated approach that manual review could not sustain.

## What We Did

**Recommendation engine**: Adapted our EARLE recommendation system (originally built for commerce in 2014) for social content discovery. The engine combines collaborative filtering (users who engage with similar content), content understanding (multimodal embeddings of video/image/text), and behavioral signals (watch time, completion rate, shares) to build personalized feeds.

**Multimodal content moderation**: Deployed Zen vision models to analyze uploaded content in real time. The system classifies content across safety categories (violence, nudity, harassment, misinformation) with human-in-the-loop review for edge cases. False positive rate under 2%.

**Creator tools**: AI-assisted content creation powered by Zen models — automatic caption generation, hashtag suggestions, optimal posting time recommendations, and thumbnail generation for video content.

**Infrastructure**: The entire AI pipeline runs on Hanzo Cloud through the LLM Gateway, processing 12M daily content items with sub-200ms inference latency.

## Results

- **400% increase** in content engagement
- **85% reduction** in harmful content reach
- **3x improvement** in feed relevance scores (user survey)
- **12M daily content items** processed through AI pipeline
- **60% reduction** in moderation costs
