# Hanzo CRM: Modern Customer Relationship Management

Salesforce is bloated. HubSpot is a marketing tool pretending to be a CRM. Pipedrive is simple but limited. Every CRM on the market was designed for a world where data entry was the job. In 2023, data entry is the obstacle.

Today we are launching Hanzo CRM: a modern CRM that captures data automatically and lets your team focus on relationships.

## What Makes It Different

### AI-Powered Data Capture

Stop typing. Hanzo CRM captures contact and deal information automatically:

- Email conversations parsed for contact details, commitments, and next steps
- Calendar events linked to deals automatically
- Call transcripts summarized with action items extracted
- LinkedIn profile data enriched automatically

Your team sells. The CRM fills itself in.

### Pipeline Management

Visual deal pipeline with drag-and-drop:

- Custom pipeline stages per deal type
- Automated stage progression based on activity
- Win probability scoring updated in real time
- Revenue forecasting with confidence intervals

### Contact Intelligence

Every contact enriched with context:

- Company information (size, industry, funding, tech stack)
- Social profiles and recent activity
- Relationship mapping (who knows whom)
- Engagement scoring based on interaction history

### Custom Objects

Not every business fits the contact/company/deal model:

- Define custom objects with custom fields
- Create relationships between any objects
- Custom views and filters
- API access for custom integrations

## Open Architecture

Hanzo CRM is built on open standards:

- Full REST and GraphQL API
- Webhook events for every data change
- Import/export in standard formats
- No data lock-in — your data is yours

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 3 users, 1,000 contacts
- **Team**: $15/user/month — unlimited contacts, AI features
- **Enterprise**: $40/user/month — custom objects, advanced analytics, SSO
