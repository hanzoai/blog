# Case Study: Unikoin Gold — $34.9M Token Launch

Unikrn, the esports betting platform founded by Rahul Sood (former Microsoft Ventures head), needed to launch a utility token that would power their global betting ecosystem. The technical and regulatory challenges were immense: smart contract development, KYC/AML compliance across 15+ jurisdictions, and a marketing strategy that would attract both crypto natives and esports fans.

## The Challenge

Token launches in 2017-2018 were the Wild West. Most ICOs were poorly executed, legally questionable, and technically fragile. Unikrn needed the opposite: a compliant, technically sound launch that would withstand regulatory scrutiny and serve as the foundation of a real product.

## What We Did

**Blockchain infrastructure**: Smart contract development and auditing for the UnikoinGold (UKG) ERC-20 token. We built the token distribution system with vesting schedules, lockup periods, and automated compliance checks.

**Regulatory compliance**: KYC/AML integration across 15+ jurisdictions. The system needed to handle different regulatory requirements per country while providing a seamless user experience.

**Marketing and community**: Growth strategy targeting both the crypto community and esports audience. Content marketing, influencer partnerships, and community management across Telegram, Discord, and Reddit.

## Results

- **120,000 ETH raised** in the token sale
- **300% increase** in platform engagement post-launch
- **15+ jurisdictions** supported with compliant onboarding
- **Zero regulatory actions** — fully compliant from day one
- **3 major exchange listings** within the first month

> "Hanzo understood both the technical blockchain architecture and the regulatory landscape. They didn't just build a token — they built the infrastructure for our entire platform economy." — Rahul Sood, CEO at Unikrn
