# Hanzo Payments: Unified Payment Processing for Global Commerce

Payments should be invisible. The customer clicks "buy," the money moves, the order ships. In practice, payment processing is a maze of processors, gateways, currencies, compliance requirements, and failure modes that consume engineering time disproportionate to the problem.

Today we are launching Hanzo Payments: unified payment processing that handles the complexity so you do not have to.

## The Problem We Solved

Our commerce customers were managing two to five payment processors simultaneously. One for credit cards in the US. Another for European SEPA transfers. A third for cryptocurrency. Each with its own API, webhook format, reconciliation process, and failure mode.

Hanzo Payments provides a single integration point for all of them.

## What's Included

### Intelligent Routing

Payments route automatically to the optimal processor based on:

- Card network and issuing bank
- Customer geography
- Transaction amount
- Processor availability and success rates
- Cost optimization

The routing engine learns from every transaction. Success rates improve over time without manual intervention.

### Multi-Currency

Accept payment in 135 currencies. Settle in your preferred currency. Exchange rates locked at transaction time — no surprise FX adjustments.

### Fraud Detection

ML-powered fraud detection integrated at the transaction level:

- Real-time risk scoring on every transaction
- Configurable rules engine for business-specific logic
- 3D Secure integration for high-risk transactions
- Chargeback management and dispute handling

### Subscriptions

Recurring billing built in:

- Flexible billing intervals
- Prorated upgrades and downgrades
- Dunning management for failed payments
- Usage-based billing support

### PCI Compliance

PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. Your servers never touch raw card data — tokenization happens at our edge before data reaches your systems.

- **No monthly fees**: Pay only for what you process
- **Volume discounts**: Custom rates above $100K/month
