# Hanzo Cap Table: Equity Management for Startups

Carta charges startups $3,000/year to manage a spreadsheet of who owns what. That is the core product: equity tracking. The 409A valuations, scenario modeling, and compliance features are useful — but the base product is a structured spreadsheet.

Today we are launching Hanzo Cap Table: equity management at startup-friendly pricing.

## Features

### Cap Table Management

- **Equity tracking**: Common stock, preferred stock, options, SAFEs, convertible notes
- **Vesting schedules**: Cliff, linear, custom — with automatic vesting calculations
- **Transaction history**: Complete audit trail of all equity events
- **Dilution modeling**: See how future rounds affect ownership

### Scenario Modeling

Answer the questions founders actually ask:

- What does ownership look like after a Series A at various valuations?
- How much dilution do the option pool holders absorb?
- What does each stakeholder receive in an exit at different valuations?
- How do SAFEs and convertible notes convert at different caps?

### 409A Readiness

- Export cap table data in 409A-ready format
- Integration with third-party valuation providers
- Automated FMV tracking for option grants

### Stakeholder Portal

Give investors and employees visibility:

- Individual ownership view
- Vesting schedule tracking
- Document access (grant letters, stock certificates)
- Tax information and reporting

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 10 stakeholders
- **Startup**: $50/month — up to 50 stakeholders, scenario modeling
- **Growth**: $150/month — unlimited stakeholders, investor portal
- **Enterprise**: Custom — multi-entity, advanced compliance
