# Case Study: Casper — Enterprise Blockchain from Concept to Mainnet

Casper Labs engaged Hanzo for strategic technical consulting on their enterprise blockchain platform. The project spanned from architectural decisions through mainnet launch — including the pivotal decision to move from Ethereum's proof-of-work to a proof-of-stake consensus mechanism built in Rust.

## The Challenge

Enterprise blockchain adoption was stalled by two problems: performance limitations of existing networks and the complexity of smart contract development for enterprise use cases. Casper needed a blockchain that enterprises would actually use, not just pilot.

## What We Did

**Technical architecture**: Consulting on the core protocol design, including the Highway consensus protocol and the contract execution engine. We recommended the pivot to Rust — a decision that improved performance, safety, and developer experience.

**Developer ecosystem**: Helped design the developer onboarding experience and tooling. Enterprise developers needed to build on Casper without becoming blockchain experts.

**Strategic consulting**: Advised on the DEVxDAO creation — a decentralized autonomous organization for funding open-source development on the Casper network.

## Results

- **Mainnet launched** successfully in March 2021
- **DEVxDAO founded** with sustainable funding model
- **Rust language pivot** delivered 10x performance improvement
- **Enterprise adoption** by multiple Fortune 500 companies
- **$0 in security incidents** since mainnet launch

> "Hanzo's technical depth in both blockchain architecture and systems programming was invaluable. The Rust recommendation alone saved us years of development time and gave us the performance profile enterprises demand." — Mrinal Manohar, CEO at Casper Labs
