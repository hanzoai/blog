# Hanzo Dataroom: Secure Document Sharing with Analytics

Fundraising, M&A, and enterprise sales all require sharing sensitive documents with external parties. The current options: email attachments (no tracking, no control), Dropbox (consumer-grade security), or dedicated dataroom software ($500+/month).

Today we are launching Hanzo Dataroom: secure document sharing with analytics at a fraction of the incumbents' price.

## Features

### Secure Sharing

- **Granular permissions**: Per-document, per-user access control
- **Expiring links**: Access automatically revokes after a date
- **Watermarking**: Dynamic watermarks with viewer identity
- **Download control**: Allow or prevent downloads per user
- **NDA gate**: Require NDA acceptance before document access

### Document Analytics

Know exactly who looked at what:

- **Per-page tracking**: See which pages each viewer spent time on
- **Time spent**: Engagement time per document and per page
- **Access log**: Complete history of who accessed what, when
- **Heat maps**: Visual representation of engagement intensity

### Organization

- **Folder structure**: Organize documents into logical sections
- **Drag-and-drop upload**: Bulk upload with automatic formatting
- **Version control**: Update documents without breaking links
- **Full-text search**: Search across all documents in the room

## Use Cases

- **Fundraising**: Share pitch decks and financials with investors
- **M&A**: Due diligence document exchange
- **Enterprise sales**: Security questionnaires and compliance docs
- **Board management**: Board materials and meeting documents

## Pricing

- **Starter**: $29/month — 1 dataroom, 1GB storage
- **Professional**: $99/month — 5 datarooms, 50GB, analytics
- **Enterprise**: Custom — unlimited rooms, SSO, custom branding
