Quasar Preview: Next-Generation Consensus

First look at Quasar, the next evolution of Snow consensus delivering 100,000+ TPS with sub-second finality.

Three years ago, we launched Lux Network with Snow consensus, achieving sub-second finality and thousands of transactions per second. Today we preview Quasar, the next evolution that pushes performance by two orders of magnitude.

The Scaling Challenge

Snow consensus works beautifully for current load:

  • 4,500+ TPS on C-Chain
  • Sub-second finality
  • Thousands of validators

But demand is growing:

  • Real-time gaming requires 50,000+ TPS
  • High-frequency trading demands microsecond latency
  • Global adoption needs millions of TPS

Quasar is our answer.

Quasar Design Principles

1. Parallel Execution

Current model: Sequential block execution

Quasar model: Parallel execution with dependency tracking

3. State Sharding
