Lux Network's comprehensive roadmap for 2025, centered on Quasar mainnet activation and the transition to 100,000+ TPS.

2024 established Lux Network as a leading blockchain platform with post-quantum security, AI integration, and native data availability. 2025 is the year we scale to meet global demand. This roadmap outlines our path forward.

Vision: Global-Scale Infrastructure

By end of 2025, Lux Network will support:

  • 100,000+ transactions per second
  • Sub-200ms finality
